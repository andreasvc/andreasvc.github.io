Workshop Innovations in Statistical Parsing

A workshop organized in conjunction with the PhD defense of Andreas van Cranenburgh, for the dissertation entitled "Rich Statistical Parsing and Literary Language."

Topics:

  • parsing with rich grammar formalisms,
  • data-oriented parsing,
  • incorporating un/semi-supervised techniques,
  • deep learning, word embeddings, etc.

Location: Science Park 105-7, 3rd floor, seminar room, Amsterdam

Date: 1 November 2016

Organizers: Andreas van Cranenburgh, Jelle Zuidema
